Upcoming / Research

AI Options Pricing Engine

Building a machine learning-based options pricing model that combines neural network approximations of the Black-Scholes framework with real-time market microstructure data. The system will generate trading signals by identifying mispricings between model-implied fair value and live market quotes across equity options chains.

Neural Nets Pricing Model
Real-Time Market Data Feed
Python PyTorch + QuantLib
2026 Target Launch

Phase 1 — Research & Data Pipeline

Apr 14 – Apr 27

Source historical options chain data (CBOE, IBKR). Build data ingestion pipeline for OHLCV + Greeks + IV surfaces. Literature review of neural network approaches to options pricing (Deep Hedging, DeepBSDE).

Phase 2 — Black-Scholes Baseline & Feature Engineering

Apr 28 – May 11

Implement analytical Black-Scholes as baseline. Engineer features: moneyness, time-to-expiry, implied vol skew, put-call parity deviations, term structure slope, realised vs implied vol spread.

Phase 3 — Neural Network Pricing Model

May 12 – May 25

Train deep neural network (PyTorch) to learn implied volatility surfaces and price options. Replace closed-form Greeks with learned sensitivities that adapt to regime changes and volatility clustering.

Phase 4 — Signal Generation & Risk Filters

May 26 – Jun 8

Compare model-implied fair value against live bid/ask to identify mispricings. Build filters: liquidity thresholds, delta-neutral constraints, max portfolio Greeks exposure, position sizing via Kelly criterion.

Phase 5 — Walk-Forward Backtesting

Jun 9 – Jun 22

Backtest across multiple market regimes (2018–2026): COVID crash, 2022 rate hikes, 2023 AI rally, 2024 vol compression. Measure Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, win rate, and P&L attribution by strategy leg.

Phase 6 — Paper Trading & IBKR API Integration

Jun 23 – Jul 6

Connect to IBKR TWS API for real-time market data and order execution. Paper trade live signals for 2 weeks minimum. Monitor model drift, execution slippage, and latency.

Phase 7 — Live Deployment & Monitoring

Jul 7 – Jul 20

Allocate initial capital with strict risk limits. Deploy monitoring dashboard for real-time P&L, Greeks exposure, and model confidence scores. Iterate on model retraining schedule.
